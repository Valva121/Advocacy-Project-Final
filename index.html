Life through the eyes of an Immigrant

A place to learn about the immigrant experience and the way they live life differently than the rest of us.


About My Topic

The process of obtaining citizenship is not very easy for immigrants and the countries or regions that immigrants move to make it harder for them to garner opportunities whether it be for employment opportunities, educational purposes, reuniting and living with their family, etc. Just the basic needs and living conditions for immigrants need to be further improved.

What is Immigration?

Immigration is the process of moving from one country or region to another. It is a process that can be deemed necessary for many people. Some people move for safety reasons, others move to be closer to family, and friends, and others for job purposes.

The Assumptions

Some people believe that immigrants are just people who come to the US (or to other countries). Some people who are citizen-born assume that they shouldn't get close to those who don't have their green cards

The Truth

Immigration is a topic people tend to not really talk about. They don't actively talk about it, but they do talk about it in the news and in the media, but it's portrayed in a negative way.In fact, they come here for a completely different reason. They come here to escape and become citizens, and they are not the same as people who are citizens.

The Solution

The solution to this problem is to educate people about the immigrant experience and the current system is not that well accommodating to immigrants and a change is needed in the system.
